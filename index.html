The Impact Of Waste On Our Community



Health Risk

Waste spreads germs and diseases that can harm people.


Pollution

Waste pollutes air, water, and soil, damaging the environment.


Plastic Overuse

Too much plastic takes a very long time to break down.


Toxic Waste

Some waste contains harmful chemicals that damage nature.

The Importance of Smart Waste Management

Smart waste management matters because it enables communities to manage waste in a safer and more efficient manner through the use of technology and improved systems.

Smart waste management ensures that waste is managed in an organized and responsible way, supporting everyday activities and long-term progress while promoting a more sustainable environment for future generations.
